Red Rock Adventures

The Plan

Picture this: it's early 2020, there are only slight hints of the chaos to come. No one has predicted the end of the world as we know it ,so what better time for a roadtrip! As avid explorers, my boyfriend and I planned my first ever visit to the magical red rocks of eastern Utah. Driving the scenic route we made a few very important stops along the way (did I just see an alpaca farm!? Pull over!). As March approched, right at the cusp of disaster, we departed and began our adventure. Final destination: Moab, Utah.
